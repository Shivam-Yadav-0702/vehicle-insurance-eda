# 🚗 Vehicle Insurance — EDA Project

## 📌 Objective
Analyze vehicle insurance dataset to understand customer behavior,
identify key factors influencing insurance purchase decisions
and generate business insights for targeted marketing.

## 📊 Analysis Areas
- Missing Value Analysis & Data Cleaning
- Response (Target Variable) Distribution
- Age & Gender Analysis
- Annual Premium Distribution & Outlier Detection
- Vehicle Age vs Insurance Interest
- Vehicle Damage Impact on Purchase Decision
- Previously Insured Customer Behavior
- Region-wise Customer Distribution
- Correlation Heatmap

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Customers with previous vehicle damage are significantly more
  likely to purchase insurance
- Middle-aged customers show higher insurance interest vs younger users
- Previously insured customers rarely respond to new insurance offers
- Older vehicles have higher insurance interest rates
- Annual premiums contain outliers common in insurance datasets
- Clear class imbalance — most customers are not interested

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns on customers with damaged vehicles
- Target middle-aged users (30-50) for better conversion rates
- Create special offers for owners of older vehicles (2+ years)
- Use insurance history for customer segmentation strategy
- Avoid targeting previously insured customers with same offerings
